About US
With the availability of numerous paid and free resources on the internet, it becomes overwhelming for students to learn new skills. AlphaGrad aims to bridge the gap between top MOOCs like Coursera, Udacity, EdX, medium article tutorials, and students eager to learn new skills. One only has to enter their level and the skill they want to learn, and AlphaGrad presents the Top 5 relevant courses and medium articles carefully picked from various sites to them.
